# Customer-Shopping-Analytics-Dashboard-SQL-Power-BI
End-to-End Data Analytics Project that analyzes customer shopping behavior using MySQL for data analysis and Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization. The project transforms raw customer shopping data into meaningful business insights through SQL queries, KPIs, and dynamic dashboards.

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior to help businesses understand sales performance, customer demographics, shopping preferences, and purchasing trends.

The analysis was performed using MySQL, while the interactive dashboard was built in Power BI to provide business stakeholders with actionable insights.

🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate a large amount of customer transaction data but often struggle to convert it into actionable insights.

This project answers important business questions such as:

How much total revenue has been generated?
Which product category generates the highest sales?
Which gender contributes more to revenue?
Which season records the highest sales?
How do subscribed customers spend compared to non-subscribers?
Which shipping method performs best?
Which payment methods are most popular?
What is the customer age distribution?
How frequently do customers purchase?
Who are the highest spending customers?

📌 Key Business Insights
Clothing generated the highest sales revenue among all product categories.
Male customers contributed approximately 68% of total sales.
Fall season recorded the highest overall sales.
Free Shipping generated the highest revenue among shipping methods.
Credit Card, PayPal, and Cash were the most preferred payment methods.
Most customers were aged 55+, representing the largest customer segment.
Non-subscribed customers generated significantly higher total sales than subscribed customers.
Customer purchases were evenly distributed across different purchase frequencies.
